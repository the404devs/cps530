Airplanes

Owen Goodwin - 500909196

Airplanes are large machines that can fly through the skies. They are mostly made of metal, and use propellers or giant turbines to propel themselves through the air.

An example of an airplane.
The most common use of airplanes is for the transportation of people and goods across large distances. Air travel is the most common form of international travel. Airplanes are also used in the military, with smaller and faster variants known as "fighter jets."
